Sator Square

The Sator Square is a magical square that contains a palindrome of five Latin words: SATOR, AREPO, TENET, OPERA, and ROTAS. Each of these words can be read from left to right, right to left, top to bottom, or bottom to top.

This square has been found in different places throughout history, and various symbolic and mystical meanings have been attributed to it.

What secret does the Sator Square hold? Discover it by exploring its mysterious composition!
